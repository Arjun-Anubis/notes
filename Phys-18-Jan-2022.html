    • Degree of hottness or coldness of an object.
    • Average total kinetic energy in an object

    - It determines the direction of flow of heat

    Unit - K, C 0° C = 273k

  2. Definition of Heat

    - Sum total energy of all the molecules in a substance

    AKA: Thermal Energy, Heat Energy

    Unit - Joule, Watt-Hour, cal 1 cal = 4.186 Joule

  3. Thermal equilibrium

    • The condition when objects in contact with each other do not further exchange head it called Thermal Equilibrium
  4. Difference between heat and temperature
    No. Heat Temperature
    1. Heat is a form of energy Temperature is a degree of hotness or colness
    2. Heat is the cause of temperature Temperature is an effect of heat

  2. Reflection of light
    • /Reflection is the phenomenon in which rays of light on striking a

    surface are sent back into the same medium/

  3. Definitions of Some terms
    1. Points
      1. Point of incidence Point where the ray strikes the mirror
      2. Point of reflection Point from which the ray emerges
    2. Rays
      1. Incident ray The ray that strickes the mirror's reflecting surface
      2. Reflected ray The ray that emerges from the mirror
      3. Normal A line drawn perpendicular to the mirror at the point of incidence
    3. Angles
      1. Angle of incidence Angle between the incident ray and normal
      2. Angle of reflection Angle between the reflected ray and normal
      3. Glance angle of incidence Angle between the incident ray and mirror
      4. Glance angle of reflection Angle between the reflected ray and mirror
  4. Laws of Reflection
    1. The incident ray, the normal, and the reflected ray are all in the same plance
    2. The angle of incidence is equal to the angle if reflection
  5. Types of Images
    1. Real Image

      Can be obtained on a screen. Is inverted. Example cinema screen

    2. Virtual Image

      Can not be obtained on a screen. Is errect. Example plane mirror.
